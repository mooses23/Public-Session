You are a senior full-stack product engineer, product designer, systems architect, and ruthless startup executor.

Your task is to build V1 of a web platform for collaborative human-made music creation.

This product is NOT AI music.
Do not add AI generation, AI suggestions, AI mastering, AI remixing, AI auto-tagging, AI music assistance, or “smart” creative features.
No generative AI inputs anywhere in the music workflow.
This platform must feel human, competitive, raw, musical, and community-driven.

The concept:
One seed song is posted by the creator.
Users download the stems.
Each round allows only one specific type of musical addition.
A contributor uploads one single added musical layer as a “commit.”
The community votes on commits.
Winning commits are merged into the official version.
The public can follow the song’s version history as it evolves.

This should feel like:
- GitHub for songs
- remix culture with structure
- public competition
- “fight your way into the official track”

This should NOT feel like:
- a DAW (digital audio workstation)
- a social network clone
- an NFT/blockchain product
- an AI music app
- bloated SaaS
- a vague “creator community”

Build a focused, brutal MVP with discipline.

6. PRODUCT LOGIC / GAME LOOP
==================================================

Implement this exact flow:

STEP 1:
Admin creates a seed song and uploads initial official mix and downloadable stems.

STEP 2:
Admin opens Round 1 and defines exactly one allowed submission type.
Examples:
- percussion only
- lead guitar only
- harmony vocals only
- pad / texture only

STEP 3:
Contributors download stems and submit one audio layer.

STEP 4:
Public listens and votes.

STEP 5:
Round closes automatically at deadline.

STEP 6:
Admin reviews top commits and chooses one or more commits to merge.

STEP 7:
Admin uploads updated official version and publishes a version history entry.

STEP 8:
Next round opens.

This loop must be clear in database design, UI, admin tools, and wording.
